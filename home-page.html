Paths to Power

Mapping the Lives of Black Women Activists of the Civil Rights Era

Paths to Power is a grant-making program and interactive platform that honors the legacy of Black women who built communities, created joy, and fought for justice during the Civil Rights era. Engage with the project in three ways:

  • For stewards of historic spaces: Apply for grants to digitize archives documenting the activism of Black women and femmes.
  • For the public and researchers: Access digitized archives online, uncovering the stories of transformative movements.
  • For community members and history enthusiasts: Join walking tours that connect participants with the spaces and narratives of this powerful history.
