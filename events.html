January 5th, 2025

Walking through Mae Mallory’s Legacy

This guided tour will focus on the extraordinary legacy of Mae Mallory. Beginning at P.S. 10 in Harlem, where Mallory’s children attended school, the walk will explore key sites of the "Harlem 9" school boycotts and locations where Mallory and fellow activists planned their groundbreaking campaigns against school segregation in New York City.

February 19th, 2025

Narratives of Everyday Resistance

Join us for the Living Histories Tour, where community voices bring the stories of unsung women activists to life. Hear personal accounts, oral history clips, and reflections from guest speakers whose families were connected to the Civil Rights Movement. Celebrate the everyday resistance that shaped our neighborhoods and continues to inspire change.
