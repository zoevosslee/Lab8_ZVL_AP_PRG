2nd February 2025

Image Credit: New York Times, 1957

This guided tour will focus on the extraordinary legacy of Mae Mallory. Beginning at P.S. 10 in Harlem, where Mallory’s children attended school, the walk will explore key sites of the "Harlem 9" school boycotts and locations where Mallory and fellow activists planned their groundbreaking campaigns against school segregation in New York City.

Celebrate the everyday resistance that shaped our neighborhoods and continues to inspire change.
